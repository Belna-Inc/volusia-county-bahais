Welcome, friend

There is a place for you here.

Across Volusia County, people of every background, belief and walk of life are coming together in homes, parks and community spaces to learn, to serve, and to discover what we can build when we see one another as members of a single family.

You don’t need to be a Bahá’í to take part. Our gatherings are open to all — come exactly as you are, with your questions, your hopes, and your willingness to contribute to the life of our community.

The Bahá’í Faith

A faith built on oneness.

Founded by Bahá’u’lláh in the nineteenth century, the Bahá’í Faith is one of the world’s youngest independent religions. Its purpose is the unity of all people and the building of an ever-advancing civilization, resting on three simple, world-embracing truths.
